Check Your English Level

Not sure where to start? Take our free placement test. It takes only 20 minutes to know your level!

  • Make sure you are in a quiet place.
  • The test has 40 questions (grammar, vocabulary, and reading).
  • You will see your result instantly after submitting.

Question 1: Choose the correct past simple tense.

Question 2: Select the correct synonym for 'Diligent'.

🎉 Congratulations!

Your English level is Intermediate (B1).
